PagerDuty Outage Notifications
==============================

Receive notifications if there is an outage with PagerDuty's services

PagerDuty Status Page
=====================

If there is a disruption to PagerDuty, we will notify customers on our company status page at [status.pagerduty.com]. During an outage, our status page will host the most up-to-date information about incidents and affected product areas. You can [subscribe to our status page](#subscribe-to-the-pagerduty-status-page) to receive email, Slack, or webhook notifications.

Subscribing to our status page will allow you to receive notifications when:

* PagerDuty posts outage incidents and updates, and/or
* PagerDuty publishes a postmortem analysis of an outage

PagerDuty Status Page Service Descriptions
==========================================

During a PagerDuty outage, you will see affected services on our [status page](https://status.pagerduty.com). Review the descriptions below for more information about which technologies fall into each category.

> ðŸ“˜ Service Regions
> -----------------
> 
> PagerDuty accounts fall under one of two [service regions](/main/docs/service-regions): the US service region and EU service region. Most Business Services on the PagerDuty Status Page have both an US and EU version, signified by **(US)** and **(EU)** respectively. These distinctions refer to the service region of the PagerDuty account as a whole, and not to individual users. For example, it is possible for users in the EU to be on a US service region account, and vice versa.

| Service | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Events API** | A disruption affecting the Events API [v2](https://developer.pagerduty.com/api-reference/YXBpOjI3NDgyNjU-pager-duty-v2-events-api) or [v1](https://developer.pagerduty.com/api-reference/YXBpOjI3NDgyNjI-pager-duty-v1-events-api). |
