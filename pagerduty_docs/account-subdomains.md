Account Subdomains
==================

Change your account's subdomain

PagerDuty accounts are accessible via a personalized subdomain. For example, if your company name is EveryNine Inc., you can set your account's subdomain to `everynine.pagerduty.com`, `e9inc.pagerduty.com`, or anything else, provided that itâ€™s five or more characters long and itâ€™s not already in use. Your account's subdomain is initially configured during [trial activation](/main/docs/trial-account-onboarding#create-a-trial-account). Once configured, your account's subdomain will then prepend your account's URL:

`[YOUR-SUBDOMAIN].pagerduty.com`

If you need to change your account's subdomain from the one initially configured, you will need to perform the following:

* [Prepare to Change Your Subdomain](#prepare-to-change-your-subdomain)
* [Change Your Subdomain](#change-your-subdomain)

Prepare to Change Your Subdomain
================================

When changing subdomains, please be mindful of the following:

* Integration email addresses for your PagerDuty services will change.
* PagerDuty will not notify users of the subdomain change. You will need to communicate the change to your team so they know when and why the old subdomain will no longer be accessible.
* Although users can still receive push notifications, the mobile app will not load and users will need to log out and log back in.
  + Please see [Using the Mobile App](/main/docs/mobile-app#log-in-to-the-mobile-app) for more information about logging in and out of the PagerDuty app.
  + If these steps don't work, you can reset the app in your device settings:
    - **iOS:** **Settings**  **PagerDuty**  **Reset** .
    - **Android:** **Settings**  **Apps**  **PagerDuty**  **Storage & cache**  **Clear Cache**.
* If you have [Single Sign On](/main/docs/sso) enabled, you will need to adjust your identity provider settings to reflect your new PagerDuty subdomain.
* If you are using the bidirectional [Microsoft Teams integration](/main/docs/microsoft-teams), you will need to uninstall and reinstall the integration.
* There is a minimum of five characters for PagerDuty subdomains.

Recommended Preparation Steps
-----------------------------

To prevent any interruption in responders being able to act on their incidents, we recommend using the following action plan before changing your subdomain.

| Action | Additional Information |
| --- | --- |
| Notify users about the upcoming subdomain change | [Sample email template](/main/docs/account-subdomains#sample-email-communication) provided |
| Add new email integration addresses within your external tools | *Recommended to limit impact on alerting* You can add the new email integration addresses within your integrated monitoring tools before the subdomain change by anticipating the change. For example:`service-email.3q1s05nk@old-subdomain.pagerduty.com`will become `service-email.3q1s05nk@new-subdomain.pagerduty.com`. Otherwise, you can swap existing email integration addresses with new ones after the subdomain change. (**NOTE**: Any emails sent to email integration addresses with the old subdomain will not trigger incidents after the subdomain change is complete.) |
| Remove old email integration addresses from your external tools | If you added new email integration addresses prior to the subdomain change, remove old email integration addresses from existing integrations for security. |
| Change V1 REST API URL endpoints (Decommissioned October 2018) | Any existing scripts hosting v1 REST API URL endpoints need to reflect the new subdomain. |
| Change SAML endpoint URL | If SSO is configured, adjust your SAML Endpoint URL to reflect the new subdomain. |

Sample Email Communication
--------------------------

### Sample Email: Preparation for Subdomain Change

> Hi Team,
> 
> On `MM/DD/YY` at `HH:MM AM/PM PDT`, our PagerDuty subdomain will change from `xxxx`.pagerduty.com to `yyyy`.pagerduty.com. This means that our email integration addresses will change. We will be working on making these adjustments to our existing integrations and scripts.
> 
> Please note that after the subdomain change, you will still receive push notifications for your incidents, however you will need to log out and log back into the mobile app to view and take action on incidents. You will also need to use `yyyy`.pagerduty.com to log in to the web app after the subdomain change, as `xxxx`.pagerduty.com will no longer be accessible.
> 
> To log out of the mobile app:
> 
> * **iOS**: Tap the **More** icon  tap the user icon  in the top left  tap **Edit** in the upper right  then tap the remove icon .
> * **Android**: Tap the **More** icon  tap the user icon  in the top right  tap the menu  to the right of the account  then tap **Remove this Account**.
> 
> If these steps don't work, you can reset the app in your device settings:
> 
> * **iOS**: **Settings**  **PagerDuty**  Reset switch to **ON** position.
> * **Android**: **Settings**  **Apps**  **PagerDuty**  **Storage & cache**  **Clear Cache**.
> 
> Please plan on logging out and logging back into the mobile app immediately after `MM/DD/YY` at `HH:MM AM/PM PDT` to ensure that you can view and take action on your incidents via the mobile app.
> 
> Please let us know if you have any questions.

### Sample Email: After Subdomain Change is Complete

> Hi Team,

> 
> As a reminder, you must log out and log back in to the mobile app to view and take action on incidents via the mobile app. You must also access `yyyy`.pagerduty.com to go to the web app.
> 
> To log out of the mobile app:
> 
> * **iOS**: Tap the **More** icon  tap the user icon  in the top left  tap **Edit** in the upper right  then tap the remove icon .
> * **Android**: Tap the **More** icon  tap the user icon  in the top right  tap the menu  to the right of the account  then tap **Remove this Account**.
> 
> If these steps don't work, you can reset the app in your device settings:
> 
> * **iOS**: **Settings**  **PagerDuty**  Reset switch to **ON** position.
> * **Android**: **Settings**  **Apps**  **PagerDuty**  **Storage & cache**  **Clear Cache**.

Change Your Subdomain
=====================

Once you have performed the [necessary preparations](#prepare-to-change-your-subdomain) and you are ready to change your subdomain, please [contact our Support team](https://www.pagerduty.com/contact-us/#contact-support). A support agent can coordinate a date and time to change your subdomain for you. The change will be effective immediately.
