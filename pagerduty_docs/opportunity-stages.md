Opportunity Stages
==================

Track your opportunities' progress | Jeli Part of PagerDuty

Opportunity Stages provide quick visibility into an investigationâ€™s current status. You can use Opportunity Stages to easily track the progression of your incident review process.

There are five Opportunity Stages:

1. **Unassigned**: You have created a Jeli opportunity, but you have not assigned an investigator. This can indicate that no one is working on the incident review.
2. **Assigned**: You have assigned an investigator to an opportunity, showing who is accountable for its completion. Jeli automatically applies this stage once an investigator is assigned.
3. **In Progress**: The investigator has started working on the opportunity. This stage will be automatically recognized if the investigator has added a marker in the [Narrative Builder](/main/docs/narrative-builder).
4. **In Review**: The investigator is seeking feedback on the opportunity from peers that participated in the incident.
5. **Completed**: Incident responders have reviewed the opportunity and are no longer working on it.

Opportunity Stages Timeline
===========================

The Opportunity Stages timeline provides an at-a-glance view of each Jeli opportunityâ€™s status. The timeline is displayed at the top of each opportunity page, highlighting the currently active stage. This can be particularly helpful for executives who want to quickly assess an opportunity's progress.

Manually Move Opportunity Stages
================================

When viewing an opportunity, you can manually update the opportunity stage from the timeline. You can click **Previous Step**, **Next Step** or the name of the stage you want to move to.

Filter by Opportunity Stage
===========================

You can use the filter on the **Opportunities** page to select opportunities in a specific stage. This can help you see which opportunities may need additional attention or resources.

1. Navigate to **Opportunities** and click **Add Filter**.
2. Select a **Dimension** to filter by:
   
   * Stage
   * Tags
   * Start date
   * Key role
   * Participants
   * Duration
   * Event or note content
3. Depending on the dimension you select, enter any additional required criteria.
4. Click **OK**.

After you have created a filter, *optionally* click  to add more filters. Click **Clear all filters** to reset your view.

FAQ
===

Expand/Collapse All

Can I customize Opportunity Stages?
-----------------------------------

No, Opportunity Stages are not customizable.

Are there any restrictions when moving from one stage to another?
-----------------------------------------------------------------

There are no restrictions when manually moving between stages and you may move to any stage at any time.
