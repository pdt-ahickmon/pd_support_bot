Jeli Learning Center
====================

Discover how incident response is impacting your organization | Jeli Part of PagerDuty

The Learning Center provides a high-level overview of the nature of incidents and incident response across your organization. By focusing on participation and on-call patterns, the Learning Center helps surface [opportunities'](/main/docs/opportunities) impact on people, systems and technology.

> 📘 Availability
> --------------
> 
> The Jeli Learning Center is available with the Enterprise for Incident Management pricing plan.
> 
> Starting the week of February 3, 2025, customers on Professional and Business pricing plans may have access to the Jeli Learning Center. We are slowly releasing this feature in a phased manner. If you do not see the Jeli Learning Center in your account, it may not yet be available.
> 
> Please [contact our Sales Team](mailto:sales@pagerduty.com) if you would like to upgrade to a plan featuring Jeli.

Filter Data
===========

You can use the following filters to adjust the Learning Center visualizations:

* **Time Frame**: Select a **time frame** from the right-hand dropdown to expand or limit opportunities included in the Learning Center visualizations. This will allow you to switch between viewing current and long-term trends in opportunity data. You can select from **Today**, **Since yesterday**, **Last 7 days**, **Last 30 days**, **Last year**, or **All time**.
* **Opportunity Tags**: Click **Add tag(s)** and select the desired opportunity tags to filter the opportunity data. These tags are additive, so the more tags you add to your filters, the larger the data set will be.

Participation Patterns
======================

Distribution of Opportunities
-----------------------------

This visualization displays the impact of opportunities on the people in your organization, and the dates and times that opportunities occur most frequently. A darker square indicates more opportunities occurred in that time frame. You can also see which opportunities occurred during your working hours, which are marked by a yellow outline.

![](https://files.readme.io/08f1381-heat_map.png)

Click on any square in the heat map to see a list of the opportunities that occurred on that date and time.

### Change Working Hours

Working hours are defined as 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. The opportunity distribution chart is based off of the timezone dropdown in the top menu bar. You can change your timezone to view the distribution of opportunities in other timezones.

Opportunity Participants
------------------------

### Who participated in the most opportunities?

This visualization displays who is most frequently involved in opportunities, and when they’re participating relative to their working hours. You can perform the following actions within this chart:

* Hover over an individual’s **name** to see more information about them. Click on their **name** to navigate to their [profile](https://support.pagerduty.com/main/docs/view-people-in-jeli#people-profile).
* Hover over their **participation bar** to see a breakdown of opportunities that occurred during and outside of working hours.
* Click on an individual’s **total opportunity count** to see a list of the opportunities they responded to within the selected time frame.

![Opportunity participants](https://files.readme.io/97f0f36-participants.png)



Opportunity participants



Participant Tenure Distribution
-------------------------------

### What was the distribution of tenure among participants?

Use this visualization to observe how participation levels fluctuate across various tenure levels within your organization. You can also determine how frequently participants engage when they are not on call during the specified time period.

![Tenure distribution](https://files.readme.io/0822c67-distribution.png)



Tenure distribution

> ------
> 
> If you have not [imported HR Data](https://support.pagerduty.com/main/docs/import-hr-data) yet, you will see a prompt to complete this step first.

Opportunity Tag Distribution
----------------------------

This visualization displays which [opportunity tags by type](https://support.pagerduty.com/main/docs/opportunity-tags#opportunity-tag-types) are most frequently associated with opportunities (e.g., What **services** were the most involved in opportunities?). You can perform the following actions within this chart:

* Toggle between **tag types** using the dropdown in the chart title.
* Click on the **total opportunity count** to see a list of the opportunities associated with each item within the selected time frame.

![Opportunity tag distribution](https://files.readme.io/a1214e3-tags.png)



Opportunity tag distribution



On Call Patterns
================

Opportunities with Participants not On Call
-------------------------------------------

Use this visualization to see which opportunities had the highest levels of participation from people who were not on call. You can perform the following actions within this chart:

* Click the opportunity **name** to view the [opportunity report](https://support.pagerduty.com/main/docs/opportunity-report).
* Click the **participant total** to see a list of the participants who were not on call.
* Click the **participant list** at the bottom to see more information about participants who most commonly appeared in the opportunities.

![Opportunities with participants not on call](https://files.readme.io/c4b50b2-on_call.png)



Opportunities with participants not on call



Participant Activity While not On Call
--------------------------------------

* Hover over an individual’s **name** to see more information about them. Click on their name to navigate to their [profile](https://support.pagerduty.com/main/docs/view-people-in-jeli#people-profile).
* Click on the individual’s **total opportunity count** to see a list of the opportunities they responded to within the selected time frame.
